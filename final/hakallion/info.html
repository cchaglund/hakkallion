HAKALLION - Solutions of the future, today!

Here at Hakallion we realize that futuristic solutions to everyday issues is no longer a matter of practicality, but a mere matter of vision. Our company's conceptual idea is to streamline cutting edge technology and functionality into mainstream everyday life - not only as an acceptable solution - but by spearheading a new praxis in civil society as we know it. This product is therefore not in itself a means to end, but rather only a small cog in our future vision. This project will utilize drones for a somewhat mundane issue och item transport, but why stop there? Perhaps soon the drone, rather than transporting what you need to you, will transport you to where you need to be. When it comes to our ambition, we have nothing to lose but our chains!

The Hakallion Helper Drone Project

This pilot project, while designed to be a independent enterprise both in vision and business model, is devised as a lighthearted, fun way to utilize and promote our newly constructed drones soon available on the consumer market. The drones currently in use are the Hummingbird 3X20F, the Homing Bird 4X40G and the White Eagle 5X5Ti (more info on the drone tab) while the current items provided are just one of many soon-to-come packages to showcase the blending of technological solutions for ordinary headaches of an urbanized lifestyle. For fun, parties or actual need on a rough sunday morning, our hangover care packages is just the first step in a fully interactive, fast delivery service fit for the new millenium.
